Modal Two

Mouse Images

A mouse is a pointing input device used to control the cursor on a computer screen. It helps users interact with files, folders, applications, and other on-screen objects by moving the pointer, clicking, dragging, and scrolling.

A standard mouse usually has a left button, a right button, a scroll wheel, and an optical sensor. The left button is used to select and open items, the right button displays shortcut menus, and the scroll wheel allows users to move up and down through documents and web pages.

There are different types of mice, including wired, wireless, optical, laser, and gaming mice. A mouse makes it easier to navigate a computer, perform tasks quickly, and improve overall productivity.

Keyboard Images

A keyboard is an input device used to enter text, numbers, symbols, and commands into a computer. It is one of the most important hardware devices and is commonly used for typing documents, browsing the internet, programming, and operating software.

Keyboards are available in different types, including wired, wireless, mechanical, membrane, ergonomic, and gaming keyboards. They improve productivity by allowing fast and accurate data entry and by supporting useful keyboard shortcuts

In simple words, a keyboard is an essential input device that helps users communicate with and control a computer efficiently.

Modal four

LAptops Speakers

Speakers are output devices that convert digital audio signals into sound. They allow users to listen to music, watch movies, attend online meetings, play games, and hear system notifications from a computer or laptop.

Speakers are available in different types, including wired, wireless, Bluetooth, portable, and multimedia speakers. They come in various sizes and power levels to suit home, office, and entertainment needs. Many modern speakers also include features such as high-quality stereo sound, deep bass, volume controls, and Bluetooth connectivity.

Computer speakers enhance the overall multimedia experience by delivering clear and powerful audio. Whether for work, learning, or entertainment, they provide an enjoyable and immersive listening experience.
